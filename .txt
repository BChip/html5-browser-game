Top-down 3v3 Multiplayer Arena Game
    -not call of duty style
    -Fast paced 
    -Punishable for running and gunning - Like cs:go
One Map, 3 champions

Mage - Fire Blast, Arcane Blast, Healing Blast
Warrior - Strike, Fury Spin, Rage
Range - Single Shot, Rapid Shot, Healing Shot

Buffs:
    -Mana regenerator
    -Hp regenerator
    -Gold Earning Increaser
    -Damage over time buff on auto attack
    -
    
    
Powerups:
    -Random Powerups are generated across the map
    -These include: Health Regen, Mana Regen, Team Attack Boost
    
Map is circular with 6 pillars
Dragon in middle
Activate 6 pillars for dragon to be on your side for 20 seconds
